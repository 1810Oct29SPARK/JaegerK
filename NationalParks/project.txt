7 Use Cases for my National Parks Website:
	1. Obtain information for planning a road trip
	2. Find hiking trails (using a map)
	3. Find general information about National Parks (what qualifies 
a National Park)
	4. Buy merchandise (t-shirts, cups, posters, etc.)
	5. Checking current conditions of the parks/trails in the parks 
(forest fires, road closures, weather conditions, etc.)
	6. Read blogs from people who have visited each park
	7. Donate money to the parks
