The theme of my SPARK project is Yosemite National Park.

7 Use Cases for my website:
	1. Obtain information for planning a trip (how much it costs, 
how long it will take to visit each site, etc.)
	2. Find hiking trails
	3. Find information about camping
	4. Find viewpoints
	5. Checking current conditions of the park (weather conditions, alerts)
	6. Buy merchandise (t-shirts, hats, etc.)
	7. Donate money to the park
